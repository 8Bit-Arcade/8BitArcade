### 1. Tournament Entry Fees (50% burned)
- Weekly tournament: 100 8BIT entry ‚Üí 50 8BIT burned
- Monthly championship: 500 8BIT entry ‚Üí 250 8BIT burned

### 2. Marketplace Fees (100% burned)
- NFT badge sales: 2.5% fee burned
- Profile customization: All fees burned

### 3. Premium Features (75% burned)
- Username changes: 1,000 8BIT ‚Üí 750 burned
- Special badges: 500 8BIT ‚Üí 375 burned

### 4. Staking Penalties (100% burned)
- Early unstaking: Penalty burned

**Estimated burn rate:** 10,000-20,000 8BIT/day
**Net emission (Year 1):** 130,000-140,000 8BIT/day
