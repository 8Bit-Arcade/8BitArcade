### How Rankings Are Determined

**Highest score wins:**
- Only your BEST score counts (not total/average)
- Unlimited attempts per day
- Play as many times as you want
- Score improves = ranking improves

**Tiebreakers:**
- If two players have same score
- Player who achieved score first ranks higher
- Encourages playing early and often

## Daily Reset Cycle

### Reset Time: 00:00 UTC Daily

**What happens at reset:**

1. **Final rankings locked** for previous day
2. **Rewards calculated** for top 10 in each game
3. **Payouts distributed** automatically to wallets
4. **Leaderboards clear** for new day
5. **New competition begins** immediately

### Understanding UTC Time

- **UTC** = Coordinated Universal Time
- **00:00 UTC** = Midnight in London
- Convert to your timezone:
  - New York: 00:00 UTC = 8:00 PM (previous day)
  - Los Angeles: 00:00 UTC = 4:00 PM (previous day)
  - Tokyo: 00:00 UTC = 9:00 AM
  - Sydney: 00:00 UTC = 11:00 AM (next day)

**Check current UTC time:** [time.is/UTC](https://time.is/UTC)

### Reset Strategy

**Play near reset time:**
- Less time for others to beat you
- Reduces competition
- Strategic for securing top 10

**Play early in cycle:**
- Set the pace
- See what score to beat
- Multiple attempts throughout day

## Competitive Strategies

### Strategy 1: Off-Peak Playing

**Play during low-traffic hours:**
- Early morning UTC (fewer players)
- Weekday afternoons
- Less competition = easier to rank

**Benefits:**
- Lower score thresholds
- Easier top 10 qualification
- More consistent earnings

### Strategy 2: Last-Minute Push

**Play right before reset:**
- See what score you need to beat
- Less time for others to overtake you
- Clutch performance under pressure

**Risks:**
- High stress
- Connection issues near deadline
- May not have time for multiple attempts

### Strategy 3: Early Dominance

**Set high score early in cycle:**
- Psychological advantage
- Forces others to chase your score
- More attempts throughout day
- Can defend position if threatened

### Strategy 4: Score Tracking

**Monitor leaderboard throughout day:**
- Track #10 score (minimum to earn)
- Track #1 score (goal to beat)
- Play only when you can improve
- Save energy for when needed

## Common Questions

### Can I rank in multiple games?

**Yes!** Each game has separate leaderboards. You can rank top 10 in all games and earn from each.

### What if I tie with someone?

**Tiebreaker:** Player who achieved the score first ranks higher.

### How often can I play?

**Unlimited!** Play as many times as you want. Only your highest score counts.

### Do I need to play every day?

**No.** Leaderboards reset daily, but you can play only when you want. You only earn rewards for days you rank top 10.

### Can I lose a rank after earning it?

**Yes.** If someone beats your score, they take your rank. Rankings update in real-time until reset.

### What happens if fewer than 10 players play a game?

All rewards still distributed proportionally to players who did play that game.

### Are tournament scores included?

**No.** Tournament scores are separate. Leaderboards are free-to-play only.
