# Setting Your Username

## Customize Your 8-Bit Arcade Identity

By default, leaderboards show your wallet address (e.g., `0x1234...5678`). Set a custom username to make your identity memorable and easier to recognize!

## How to Set Your Username

### Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

1. Visit **[play.8bitarcade.games](https://play.8bitarcade.games)**
2. Click **"Connect Wallet"**
3. Approve connection in MetaMask
4. Ensure you're on **Arbitrum One** network

### Step 2: Access Profile Settings

1. Click your **wallet address** (top right corner)
2. Select **"Profile Settings"** from dropdown
3. Username section will appear

### Step 3: Choose Your Username

**Username Requirements:**
- 3-20 characters long
- Letters, numbers, underscores only
- No spaces or special characters
- Case-insensitive (PixelKing = pixelking)
- Must be unique (first come, first served)

**Good Examples:**
- ‚úÖ `PixelMaster`
- ‚úÖ `ArcadeKing_420`
- ‚úÖ `CryptoGamer2024`
- ‚úÖ `8BitChamp`

**Bad Examples:**
- ‚ùå `PK` (too short)
- ‚ùå `ThisUsernameIsTooLongForTheSystem` (too long)
- ‚ùå `Pixel King` (space not allowed)
- ‚ùå `Pixel@King` (special character not allowed)

### Step 4: Check Availability

1. Enter desired username
2. Click **"Check Availability"**
3. System checks if username is taken
4. Green checkmark = available ‚úÖ
5. Red X = already taken ‚ùå

### Step 5: Confirm Transaction

1. Click **"Set Username"**
2. MetaMask popup appears
3. Review transaction details:
   - **Cost:** Small gas fee (~$0.20)
   - **Action:** Register username on-chain
4. Click **"Confirm"**
5. Wait for transaction to confirm (~5 seconds)
6. Success! Username is now set

## Username Costs

### First-Time Setup

- **Gas fee only:** ~$0.20 (Arbitrum gas)
- **No registration fee**
- **Free username claim**

### Changing Username

If you want to change your username later:

- **Small fee:** 1,000 8BIT (~$0.30 at $0.0003)
- **Plus gas:** ~$0.20
- **Prevents spam** and username squatting
- **Old username becomes available** for others

## Username Rules & Policies

### Prohibited Usernames

Usernames that are **offensive, racist, sexual, or violate terms** will be:
- Flagged for review
- Reset to wallet address
- Subject to account restrictions

**Examples of prohibited usernames:**
- Racial slurs or hate speech
- Sexual/NSFW content
- Impersonation of team/staff
- Scam-related terms (e.g., "Support", "Admin")

### Squatting Prevention

- No registration fees for first username
- Change fees prevent rapid cycling
- Inactive accounts (6+ months) may have usernames freed
- Can't reserve multiple usernames with one wallet

### Verification Badges (Future)

Planned features for verified accounts:

- ‚úÖ **Blue Checkmark** - Verified content creators
- ‚úÖ **Gold Checkmark** - Top 10 all-time players
- ‚úÖ **Team Badge** - Official 8-Bit Arcade team

## Managing Your Username

### View Current Username

1. Click wallet address (top right)
2. See current username displayed
3. Click to copy for sharing

### Change Username

1. Go to **Profile Settings**
2. Click **"Change Username"**
3. Enter new username
4. Pay change fee (1,000 8BIT + gas)
5. Confirm transaction
6. New username active immediately

### Revert to Wallet Address

If you want to stop using a username:

1. Go to **Profile Settings**
2. Click **"Remove Username"**
3. Confirm (free, gas only)
4. Leaderboards will show wallet address again
5. Username becomes available for others

## Technical Details

### On-Chain Registry

Usernames are stored on the Arbitrum blockchain:

- **Smart contract:** UsernameRegistry.sol
- **Storage:** Mapping of address ‚Üí username
- **Immutable history:** All changes recorded
- **Decentralized:** No central database

### Privacy Considerations

**Usernames are public:**
- Anyone can see your username
- Linked to your wallet address
- Visible on blockchain explorer
- Cannot be made private

**If you value privacy:**
- Use a pseudonym, not real name
- Don't link to other identities
- Consider separate wallet for gaming

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is username required to play?**
A: No, it's completely optional. Wallet address will show if no username is set.

**Q: Can two people have the same username?**
A: No, usernames are unique. First person to claim it owns it.

**Q: Can I change my username later?**
A: Yes, for a small fee (1,000 8BIT + gas).

**Q: What if someone takes my desired username?**
A: Try variations: add numbers, underscores, or slight changes.

**Q: Are usernames case-sensitive?**
A: No. "PixelKing" and "pixelking" are treated the same.

**Q: Can I sell my username?**
A: Usernames are non-transferable and tied to your wallet address.

**Q: What happens if I lose access to my wallet?**
A: You lose access to your username. It remains tied to that wallet forever.

**Q: Can I have multiple usernames?**
A: No, one username per wallet address.

**Q: Will there be a username marketplace?**
A: Not currently planned, but may be considered for future updates.
