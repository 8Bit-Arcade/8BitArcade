## How Tournaments Work

### Entering a Tournament

1. **Browse Tournaments**
   - Visit **[play.8bitarcade.games/tournaments](https://play.8bitarcade.games/tournaments)**
   - See all active and upcoming tournaments
   - Filter by entry fee, game, prize pool

2. **Select Tournament**
   - Click on tournament you want to join
   - Review details:
     - Entry fee
     - Prize breakdown
     - Duration
     - Current participants
     - Game rules

3. **Pay Entry Fee**
   - Click **"Enter Tournament"**
   - Approve USDC spend in MetaMask
   - Confirm transaction
   - Entry confirmed!

4. **Play Games**
   - Play the specified game
   - Submit scores during tournament period
   - Unlimited attempts (only best score counts)
   - Track your ranking in real-time

5. **Tournament Ends**
   - Final rankings locked
   - Prizes distributed automatically
   - Rewards sent to winner wallets
   - Results published

## Entry Fees & Payment

### Accepted Payment Methods

**Primary:** USDC (Stablecoin)
- Most common payment method
- No price volatility
- Easy to acquire
- Fast transactions

**Alternative:** ETH
- Automatically converted to USDC
- Market rate at time of entry
- Small conversion fee
- Convenient for ETH holders

### How to Get USDC

#### Option 1: Buy on Exchange

1. Use Binance, Coinbase, Kraken, etc.
2. Buy USDC
3. Withdraw to Arbitrum network
4. Arrives in your wallet

#### Option 2: Swap in Wallet

1. Have ETH or other tokens
2. Use Uniswap or built-in wallet swap
3. Swap to USDC on Arbitrum
4. Pay in tournament

#### Option 3: Bridge from Ethereum

1. Have USDC on Ethereum mainnet
2. Use [bridge.arbitrum.io](https://bridge.arbitrum.io)
3. Bridge to Arbitrum One
4. Wait ~10 minutes

### Transaction Costs

**Entry Fee:** $10 tournament

**Total Cost:** ~$10.20

Very affordable compared to Ethereum mainnet ($5-$50 gas fees).

## Tournament Strategies

### Strategy 1: Selective Entering

**Don't enter every tournament:**
- Choose games you're best at
- Enter when prize pool justifies risk
- Skip tournaments with too many participants
- Focus on events with less competition

### Strategy 2: Early Bird Advantage

**Enter and play early:**
- Set the pace with high score
- Psychological pressure on others
- More time to defend position
- See competition develop

### Strategy 3: Last-Minute Clutch

**Wait until final hours:**
- See what score you need to beat
- One perfect run can win
- Less time for others to respond
- High-pressure, high-reward

### Strategy 4: ROI Focus

**Calculate expected value:**

Example:
- $10 entry fee
- 100 participants = $500 prize pool
- You estimate 20% chance to place top 3
- Expected value = (Prize if win Ã— Probability) - Entry fee

**Only enter when EV is positive.**

### Strategy 5: Practice First

**Before entering:**
- Play game extensively in free mode
- Know your skill ceiling
- Understand what score is competitive
- Enter only when confident
